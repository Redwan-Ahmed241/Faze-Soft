# FazeSoft

**FazeSoft** is a software development company focused on building scalable, high-quality digital products for startups and businesses. We partner with clients from concept all the way through to deployment, acting as a full-stack development team under one roof.

## 🏢 Who We Are

We are a team of engineers and designers passionate about turning ideas into working software. With experience across web, mobile, and enterprise platforms, we bring both technical depth and product thinking to every project we take on.

## 💡 What We Do

- **Custom Software Development** — Tailored solutions built around your business logic, integrations, and growth goals.
- **Web Application Development** — Modern, responsive web apps and SaaS platforms using React and REST/GraphQL APIs.
- **Mobile App Development** — Cross-platform mobile experiences with a native look and feel on iOS and Android.
- **UI/UX Design** — User-centred design that takes a product from wireframe to polished, production-ready interface.

## 🚀 Why This Repo Exists

This repository contains the source code for the **FazeSoft company website** — our public-facing presence where potential clients can learn about our services, browse past projects, read our blog, and get in touch.

The site is designed to showcase:

- Who we are and what we stand for
- The services we offer and the technologies we use
- A portfolio of 15+ real projects delivered across multiple industries
- A way for new clients to reach us directly
